About Me





Hi! My name is ChanChan Mao. I was born and raised in San Mateo, California, but I recently moved to Westfield, New Jersey in September 2019. I attend University of California, Santa Barbara and I am majoring in Computer Science.

I have worked at Timothy Adams Chocolates, a cute little chocolate shop located in Palo Alto, CA, for the past two years since summer of 2017. After starting college, I could not work anymore, so I like to go back every summer after every school year to help out where I can.
